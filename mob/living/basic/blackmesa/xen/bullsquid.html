Bullsquid (/mob/living/basic/blackmesa/xen/bullsquid) An aggressive alien creature from Xen that uses both melee and ranged attacks.

A hostile mob that switches between biting nearby targets and spitting acid at distant ones. Uses standard melee attacks when adjacent and acid spit when at range. Each bullsquid is hostile to other bullsquids, creating organic free-for-all combat.
