1. INTRODUCTION AND SCOPE

This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) outlines the policies and procedures of Bubberstation (“we,” “us,” “our”) regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information. This Policy applies to all individuals (“you,” “user”) who access or use any of our associated services (collectively, the “Services”).

Our Services include, but are not limited to:

    Our game server (“Game”) on the BYOND platform.
    Our official website, forums, wiki, and status page (collectively, “Website”).
    Our official Discord server (“Discord”).
    Our connected Application Programming Interface (API) and any other backend services that connect, track, or link user accounts across these platforms.

By accessing or using any of our Services, you signify your understanding of and agreement to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

In the course of operating our Services, we may collect a wide range of information, which includes:

    Identifiers and Account Information: This includes information you voluntarily provide, such as your BYOND username (CKey), Discord username and User ID, and any other credentials or identifiers used to register for or access our Services.
    Device and Hardware Information: We automatically collect data about the device, computer, and software you use to access our Services. This may include, but is not limited to, your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, and unique hardware identifiers (such as a HWID).
    Service Usage and Log Data: When you interact with our Services, our servers automatically record information. This data may include connection timestamps, in-game actions, chat logs (from the Game and Discord), IP addresses, and records of your activity on our Website.
    Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our Website. This information is used for essential site functionality, session management, and to preserve your preferences. This may include cookies that store your login state for our website or other connected services.
    Third-Party Platform Data: When you interact with our Services through third-party platforms such as BYOND, or Discord we may collect information provided to us by those platforms as part of their standard API or service functionality.

3. BASIS FOR AND PURPOSE OF DATA USE

Our processing of your personal information is for the legitimate interest of operating, maintaining, and moderating our community and Services. We do not use your data for commercial analytics, marketing, or to sell to any third party.

The purposes for which we use your information are:

    Provision and Operation of Services: To operate, maintain, and provide the full functionality of the Game, Website, and all connected Services.
    Account Management and Integration: To create and maintain your account, and to facilitate the linking of your various accounts (BYOND, Discord, etc.) via our API for integrated community features.
    Security, Integrity, and Rule Enforcement: To protect the security and integrity of our Services and community.
    Legal Compliance: To comply with any applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information. Your information is disclosed only in the following limited circumstances:

    Third-Party Service Providers: Your information is inherently processed by the third-party platforms you use to access our Services (e.g., BYOND, and Discord) and by our infrastructure providers (e.g., our servers or Website hosts). We do not control the privacy practices of these third parties.
    Authorized Volunteer Staff: Our authorized volunteer administrators and moderators may be granted access to your personal information (such as logs, IP addresses, and hardware identifiers) strictly as necessary to perform their moderation and administrative duties in accordance with our internal policies.
    Legal Compliance and Protection: We may disclose your information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with a legal obligation, (b) protect and defend our rights or property, (c) act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users or the public, or (d) protect against legal liability.

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We implement reasonable administrative and technical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We store data on secure servers, primarily located in the United States. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and to provide our Services.

    Information related to the general use of the Services, such as transient logs, may be retained for a limited period.
    Information related to your account profile may be retained for as long as your account is active.
    Data related to moderation, sanctions, rule violations, or enforcement actions (including associated identifiers like CKey, IP address, and HWID) may be retained indefinitely.

7. YOUR DATA RIGHTS AND CHOICES

We honor your rights to your data, including those provided under regulations like the GDPR. Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

    Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
    Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
    Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request the deletion of your personal information.

How to Make a Request:

To exercise any of these rights, you must contact us via the designated contact email below.

Limitations:

    Requesting deletion is irreversible and will result in the permanent removal of your account, game progress, and associated data.
    We may decline requests that are excessively repetitive, require disproportionate technical effort, or jeopardize the privacy of others.
    We cannot fulfill deletion requests for data that we are legally required to maintain.
    We will not delete data related to moderation and ban records.

8. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Our Services are not intended for or directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we may take steps to remove such information.
